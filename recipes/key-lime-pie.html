Key Lime Pie

Description

The best dessert is Key Lime Pie. That's why we have the recipe for it on this website. This is a recipe that's going to be easy to follow, and it's a simple Key Lime Pie

"This Key Lime Pie is amazing" - Guy Fieri

Ingredients

  • 5 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1 14oz can of condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup of key lime juice
  • 1 9-inch prepared graham cracker crust

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Combine sweetened condensed milk, key lime juice, and egg yolks in a large bowl; mix well. Pour into unbaked graham cracker crust.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until filling is set, about 15 minutes. Allow to cool completely before slicing.
